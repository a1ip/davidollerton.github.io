David Ollerton

David is a Welsh-speaking Lancastrian who maintains his childhood loyalty to English rugby and Manchester United. He is married to Liz, has two daughters and a son, and six grandchildren. With them, he walks up mountains… a pleasure that he is passing down the family’s genes. He also works with Waleswide/Cymrugyfan, a church planting, strengthening and training network.
